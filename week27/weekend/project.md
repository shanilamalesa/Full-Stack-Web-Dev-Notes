# Week 27 Weekend: Finalise The Spec And Tell Your Story

Light weekend. Finalise the spec, write a README pitch, and prepare a 3-minute pitch for Monday.

**Estimated time:** 2-3 hours.

---

## Tasks

- [ ] Re-read `ARCHITECTURE.md`, `API.md`, `PLAN.md` end-to-end. Fix anything that does not make sense.
- [ ] Write a `README.md` elevator pitch for the SME OS (1-2 paragraphs).
- [ ] Draft a 3-minute pitch you will give on Monday morning. Include:
  - Who the customer is.
  - What they struggle with today.
  - What the SME OS does.
  - Why it will work.
- [ ] Push the repo public (if you are comfortable). A public Capstone repo is a CV piece.

---

## Deliverables

- `README.md` with the pitch.
- A Trello/Linear board link in the README.
- A 3-minute pitch script (just notes, not a full write-up).

---

## Hints

**The pitch is not for a VC.** It is for your cohort and your instructor. Focus on the problem and the architecture, not the business model.

**Make it boring.** The Capstone is not about a clever business idea. It is about execution. "We build A for B because C" in four sentences is enough.

**Read Stripe's architecture docs.** The Stripe atlas docs are good examples of how mature companies explain their architecture. Steal their format.
